Key Takeaways

  • 2,836 tests in the first 5 months of 2026 -- already at 49.6% of the full 2025 total (5,712) with 7 months still to go
  • Feb -- May 2026: 2,473 vs 1,996 in 2025 (+23.9%) -- like-for-like comparison (excluding partial January data in both years)
  • Every month ahead of 2025: Feb +12.5%, Mar +22.1%, Apr +55.2%, May +9.9%
  • April was the standout month: 711 tests vs 458 in Apr 2025 (+55.2%) -- the biggest single-month YoY jump so far
  • May 2026 broadly flat vs Apr 2026 -- 708 vs 711 (-0.4%), but still +9.9% vs May 2025 (644)
  • Annualised 2026 pace: ~6,806 -- on track to beat 2025 by ~19%
  • 2025 peaked Jun -- Jul (748 and 736). If 2026 follows a similar seasonal pattern, the summer months should push totals well clear of 2025's pace
