Data Sources & Notes

All standard cleaning rules applied: test/null entries removed, company name consolidations applied (PF&H HQ combined, HPH split by location, etc.).

Key Takeaways

  • ▲2,473 tests in Feb -- May 2026 vs 1,996 in 2025 (+23.9%) -- month-on-month YoY: Feb +12.5%, Mar +22.1%, Apr +55.2%, May +9.9%
  • ▲132 active centres vs 97 last year (+36.1%) -- the network is expanding, not just existing centres doing more tests
  • ★PF&H HQ: 406 tests vs 221 (+83.7%) -- internal testing capacity continues to scale
  • ▲Canada: 69 vs 19 (+263.2%) -- driven by University of Alberta, Macleod Performance, and Foveo Running
  • ▲Europe: 114 vs 52 (+119.2%) -- continental expansion gaining momentum
  • ▲UK +11.5%, US +29.0% -- both core markets growing steadily
  • ▼Japan -50.0%, Australia -20.1% -- the declining regions; largely timing/seasonal shifts
  • ★Annualised 2026 pace: 6,806 tests -- on track to beat 2025's full-year total of 5,712 by ~19%
